ZOOM GUIDE

DO YOU WANT TO KNOW HOW TO USE THIS APP? Follow these simple steps below!

ABOUT MEMBERS

If you are a member wanting to join a meeting, enter your MEMBER ID and press the button below it. Next, write your name as requested. Be careful, because entering wrong information may prevent you from joining.

ABOUT MODERATOR

If you are a moderator, enter your MODERATOR ID and press the button below. Then, input your name to continue.

OTHER THINGS YOU NEED TO KNOW

Moderators can control all members, including their camera and microphone. They are also the only ones allowed to upload videos during meetings.
